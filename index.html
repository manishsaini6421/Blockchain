About

The Certificate Verification System is a secure platform designed to help you verify the authenticity of certificates quickly and easily. Whether you're an employer, educational institution, or an individual looking to confirm the validity of a certificate, our system is here to assist you.

How It Works

  1. Validate Certificate: Verify the authenticity of a certificate by entering the certificate details. Ensure that the provided document is genuine.
  2. Upload Certificate: If you are an institution or an individual, you can upload certificates to our database for easy verification by others.
